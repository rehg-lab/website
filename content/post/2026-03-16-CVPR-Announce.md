Six papers accepted for publication at the *IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition* ([CVPR 2026](https://cvpr.thecvf.com/)):
* B. Lai, X. Wang, S. Rambhatla, J. M. Rehg, Z. Kira, R. Girdhar, I. Misra. Toward Diffusible High-Dimensional Latent Spaces: A Frequency Perspective.
* X. Li, S. Deng, B. Lai, W. Pian, J. M. Rehg, Yapeng Tian. Omni-MMSI: Toward Identity-attributed Social Interaction Understanding.
* Y. Chi, X. Li, Z. Huang, J. M. Rehg. Vinedresser3D: Towards Agentic Text-guided 3D Editing.
* Z. Huang, X. Li, Z. Lv, J. M. Rehg. How Much 3D Do Video Foundation Models Encode?
* X. Cao, H. Yang, V. Gunda, Z. Zhou, T. Xu, A. Kowdle, I. Kim, J. M. Rehg. Gaze Target Estimation with Concepts.
* F. Ryan, I. Ananthabhotla, Y. Qian, J. Hoffman, J. M. Rehg, V. K. Ithapu, C. Murdock. Forecasting 3D Scanpaths in Egocentric Video.

Two Findings papers accepted at CVPR 2026:
* W. Jia, B. Lai, M. Liu, D. Xu, J. M. Rehg. Learning Predictive Visuomotor Coordination.
* B. Boote, J. Kim, O. Kara, S. Lee, J. M. Rehg CoherentHand: Temporally Consistent 3D Hand Trajectory Synthesis with Semantic Motion Priors.
